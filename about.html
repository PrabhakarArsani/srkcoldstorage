About Us

Every strong enterprise begins with a vision—and ours began in the heart of Telangana's agricultural trade.

Sai Radha Krishna Coldstorage Pvt Ltd was founded in 2005 by Mr. Konijeti Radha Krishna Murthy, a seasoned professional deeply involved in the chillies commodity trading business.

Through years of hands-on experience in agricultural markets, he witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by farmers and traders—post-harvest losses, price volatility, and the lack of reliable cold storage infrastructure.

Recognizing the immense growth potential and expansion opportunities in temperature-controlled storage, Mr. Murthy took a forward-looking step to bridge this critical gap in the supply chain. What started as a strategic move soon evolved into a pioneering venture.

Today, Sai Radha Krishna Coldstorage Pvt Ltd proudly stands as Telangana's first cold storage facility spread across 2.5 acres with a capacity of 15,000 metric tonnes, marking a significant milestone in the region's cold chain development.

A Legacy Across Generations

Built on strong values of trust, integrity, and long-term thinking, the company has grown steadily over the years. What began as a founder-led initiative is now managed by the third generation of the Konijeti family, combining legacy knowledge with modern operational practices.

This seamless blend of experience and innovation allows us to continuously adapt to changing market needs while staying rooted in our original mission—supporting agricultural and perishable goods ecosystems with dependable cold storage solutions.

Multi-Commodity Storage

From chillies and spices to fruits, vegetables, and perishable foods, our facility is engineered to handle diverse product requirements with precision.

Temperature Integrity

Chambers calibrated for both chilled and frozen products, with continuous monitoring for consistent temperature and humidity profiles.

Supply Chain Reliability

Location, infrastructure, and processes aligned to reduce spoilage, improve turnaround times, and protect value from harvest to market.

What We Do

Our facility is designed to store a wide range of temperature-sensitive commodities, including:

  • Chilli and other spices (turmeric, coriander, cumin, red chilli, cardamom, black pepper)
  • Agricultural produce (fruits, vegetables, grains)
  • Food products requiring controlled environments (dairy, meat, seafood)
  • Processed food items and bulk commodities
  • Seeds and agricultural inputs

With a focus on quality preservation, reduced spoilage, and supply chain efficiency, we help our clients maintain product value from harvest to market.

Our Commitment
