Welcome to SRK Cold Storage

Founded in 2005, Sai Radha Krishna Cold Storage is a large-scale cold storage facility serving agricultural and perishable commodity markets.

We provide temperature-controlled storage solutions designed to preserve quality and extend product shelf life.

Our focus is on reducing post-harvest losses, maintaining cold-chain integrity, and supporting a reliable agricultural supply chain.
